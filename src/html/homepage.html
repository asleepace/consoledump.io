consoledump

Welcome to consoledump.io, a free service that allows you to share your terminal sessions with anyone you want!

 
curl -X POST -H "Content-Type: application/json" -d '["Hello, world"]' https://consoledump.io/stdin

or paste the following into your browser console:

curl -X POST -H "Content-Type: application/json" -d '["Hello, world"]' https://consoledump.io/stdin
